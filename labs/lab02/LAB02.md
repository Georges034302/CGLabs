## Lab 2 Objectives
* Create a **Sun–Earth–Moon** system (Sun is fixed)
* Earth **orbits the Sun** and **rotates anti-clockwise**
* Moon **orbits the Earth clockwise**
* Moon is **tidally-locked** to Earth (same face always points to Earth)
* Add mouse controls (**OrbitControls**) to **zoom in/out** and navigate the scene
